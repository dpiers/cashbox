Create a CashBox

Creating a CashBox is easy. Sign up through Facebook, Twitter, or Google. Come up with a title and start inviting friends immediately. All CashBox payments are protected by our good buddies at PayPal.

Set Goal & Schedule

After creating your CashBox, you can set a one time amount that can be split amongst group members or you can require recurring, monthly payments. You can also create a CashBox for donations.

Notify Your Members

Instead of having to worry about reminding your friends that they owe you money, you can use CashBox to send out notifications to your group members to let them know how much they owe.
